Site Purpose

The site name will be Weather You Like It, and the domain for this site is weatheryoulikeit.com and costs $12 a year. The name "Weather You Like It" is a fun play on how weather is what it is, whether we like it or not.

The main purpose of weatheryoulikeit.com is to educate users on what the projected forcast is in an area so that they may feel prepaired, safe, and sometimes a little surprised. This purpose will be fulfilled by including an up to date 5 day forecasts, as well as articles on weather.
